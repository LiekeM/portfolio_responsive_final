OPEN_HRTG

Built with HTML and CSS

OPEN HRTG; is a digital documentation database of ancient cultural heritage. By collecting your 3D-scans of objects in musea, heritage sites and institutes, we create a free acces database to objects and places of historical value through digital technologies.

Weather App

Built with HTML and CSS

What Should I wear is a weather app, presenting the forecast in a fresh and playful manner. By displaying emojis of clothing and accesoires instead of weather icons, this app gives the user an indication of what to wear, today, tomorrow or the next five days.
